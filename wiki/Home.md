## The mechanic worth knowing

**Eight seats, and everyone else is a spirit.** The Age of Chaos is the only chapter with no
mortals in it, because there is nothing to be yet — that is the point of the Age of Chaos.

And the Titans **cannot be claimed at all**. Their rule denies everyone, always. The only way to become
Kronos is for whoever holds **Gaia** to reach into the spirit world and pull you out of it. One player's
existence is another player's power, which is what Greek myth is actually about.
